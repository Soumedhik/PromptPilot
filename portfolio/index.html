About PromptPilot

PromptPilot is a transformative application designed to help individuals articulate their thoughts during interviews or professional conversations. Powered by advanced Generative AI, it combines real-time voice transcription and response generation to assist users in confidently handling interviews and overcoming nervousness or communication barriers.

The application leverages cutting-edge technologies including Whisper distil-large-v3 for accurate voice recognition and LLaMA 3.3 for personalized response generation, creating a seamless interview preparation experience.
